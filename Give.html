Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and faith. When you give, you participate in God's work of transforming lives in our community and beyond.

Tithes

The firstfruits of what God has given us. Tithing is returning to God what already belongs to Him—a thankful acknowledgment of His provision.

"Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse..." — Malachi 3:10

Offerings

Freewill gifts above and beyond the tithe. Offerings are expressions of love and generosity that support the ongoing work of ministry.

"Each one must give as he has decided..." — 2 Corinthians 9:7

Missions & Outreach

Supporting local and global mission efforts. Your giving extends the love of Christ to those who need to hear the good news.

"Go and make disciples of all nations..." — Matthew 28:19

Questions?

Frequently Asked About Giving

Is my donation secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your financial information. Your data is never stored on our servers.

Can I set up recurring giving?

Absolutely! You can choose to give one-time or set up recurring donations weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

Will I receive a tax receipt?

Yes, you'll receive an annual tax receipt for all your donations. Please ensure you provide your email address.

Can I change my giving amount?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your recurring giving at any time through your account dashboard.
