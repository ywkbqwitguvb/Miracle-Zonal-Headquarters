What to Expect

We want your first visit to feel like coming home.

Service Length

Our services typically last about 75 minutes, including worship and a message.

Arrive Early

We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to grab coffee and find a seat.

Kids Ministry

Safe, age-appropriate programs for newborns through 5th grade during all services.

Parking

Free parking available. First-time guests get reserved spots near the entrance.

We want to make your first visit special!

Common Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans to dress clothes. We want you to feel comfortable.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself! We'll provide everything you need. Bibles are available if you'd like one.

What about my kids?

Check-in opens 15 minutes before each service. Your kids will have a blast while you enjoy the service worry-free.

Will I be singled out as a visitor?

We won't embarrass you or ask you to stand up. We'll simply be glad you're here!

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help make your visit as comfortable as possible.
