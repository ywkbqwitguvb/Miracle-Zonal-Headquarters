Our Story

Miracle Zonal Church was founded in 1998 by a small group of believers who shared a vision for a church that would be authentically welcoming to everyone, regardless of background or where they are in their spiritual journey.

What began as a gathering of 15 people in a living room has grown into a vibrant community of hundreds of families. But our heart remains the same: to help people know God, find freedom, discover their purpose, and make a difference.

Today, we're a diverse family united by our love for Jesus and our commitment to serving our city. We believe the best is yet to come.

What We Believe

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by creating environments where people are encouraged to pursue intimacy with God, community with others, and influence in the world.

Our Vision

To be a life-giving church that reaches our city, connects people to community, and transforms lives through the power of the Gospel.

Our Values

We value authentic worship, biblical teaching, genuine community, generous living, and servant leadership. These core values guide everything we do.
